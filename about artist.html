Art journey

In 2017, out of curiousity, my journey as an artist began. To me at first it was just a hobby to push time, but something suddenly changed when I came across realistic pencil drawings on youtube tutorials. It caught my eye, or should I say, 'my heart', and I began to pursue pencil art. I started to practice with simple drawings as working on lips, hair and eyes. With practice came perfection, hence I ventured into hyper-realistic pencil Art. Since then I never stopped, and each step I make I try to improve on my level.

Have you ever wondered if you could tell what a person's heart is saying. That ability is beyond the scope of humanity. But through Art it is possible. Through my journey to become an artist, I started to understand the true meaning of "Art", and what us as artists hold in our hearts and minds. Speaking to people's hearts is a huge priviledge, and that is what I strive to accomplish.

Let me speak to your heart.
